SUBJECT

Maximal Extractable Value (MEV) Practices

On Solana blockchain in context of Pump.fun memecoin launches; allegations centered on Jito Labs infrastructure and alleged coordination with Pump.fun / Solana entities

MEV TECHNICAL EXPLANATION (NEUTRAL DEFINITION)

Maximal Extractable Value (MEV)

Additional profit block producers (validators) or third parties can capture by reordering, including, or excluding transactions within a block beyond standard block rewards and fees.

MEV on Solana

  • High-throughput architecture enables rapid transaction processing
  • MEV manifests via transaction ordering advantages in mempool/leader schedule

Jito Labs Role

Provides validator client (Jito-Solana) with bundle auction system:

  • Searchers submit atomic bundles (up to 5 transactions) with tips (priority fees) to validators via off-chain auction
  • Winning bundles prioritized for inclusion based on tip amount
  • Reduces spam, reallocates MEV to validators/stakers via tips
  • Jito retains portion (e.g., 5-6% historically reported)
  • Bundles execute atomically (all-or-nothing); enables strategies like arbitrage/backrunning without partial failure risk

CONFIRMED INFRASTRUCTURE ATTRIBUTES

  • Jito infrastructure widely used on Solana for MEV optimization (bundles, tips, block engine)
  • Integrated in validator clients running Jito-Solana
  • Pump.fun operates on Solana
  • Memecoin launches involve high initial volatility and thin liquidity, making transaction ordering sensitive

ALLEGATIONS

Per plaintiffs' filings and reporting; defendants contest all claims; no liability findings as of January 31, 2026

Core Allegation

Pump.fun secretly integrated Jito MEV tools to enable insiders/affiliates to front-run retail buys during token launches via Jito bundles and tips. Insiders allegedly paid tips for priority execution, purchasing tokens at lower prices before public orders, then selling post-price rise (risk-free arbitrage claimed).

Coordination Alleged

Between Pump.fun, Solana Labs/Foundation, and Jito to exploit Solana mempool/validator infrastructure for MEV extraction

Estimated Losses

Retail losses tied to these practices estimated in billions ($4–5.5B aggregate in broader claims)

Marketing Claims

Platform marketed as "fair" launches while allegedly facilitating insider advantages through MEV mechanics

Internal Evidence

Internal chats (5,000+ leaked/referenced December 2025) purportedly show discussions of MEV tooling integration and bundle usage
