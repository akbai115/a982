SUBJECT

Pump.fun

Baton Corporation Ltd. operating entity

ECONOMIC FRAMEWORK

Bonding curve automated market maker for initial token distribution + graduated trading on PumpSwap (internal AMM on Solana)

  • Fixed 1 billion token supply per coin
  • ~800 million tokens allocated to bonding curve at launch
  • Price determined by automated curve (step-function or synthetic reserves model per public analyses)
  • Remaining tokens reserved for post-graduation liquidity

CREATOR FEE SYSTEM

Creator Fee Applicability

  • Applies to all coins on bonding curve or PumpSwap from May 13, 2025
  • Creators receive portion of trade fees ongoing
  • 0.300% of all trades directed to creator wallet(s)
