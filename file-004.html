ICO / Public Sale

Start: July 12, 2025 at 14:00 UTC

Duration: July 12–15, 2025 or until sold out

Post-Launch Distribution

Tokens initially frozen, then distributed within 48–72 hours. Trading commenced shortly after with listings on major exchanges including Bybit, KuCoin, Kraken, and others.
