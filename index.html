ABOUT

This site is an independent, public-facing research archive dedicated to documenting and contextualizing information about cryptocurrency platforms, figures, and events of public interest.

Our work focuses on verifiable facts, primary-source documentation, and clear separation between confirmed information, allegations, and opinion. We prioritize court filings, regulatory records, official statements, and reputable journalism. Unverified rumors and anonymous speculation are intentionally excluded.

The purpose of this archive is documentation, not advocacy. We do not make legal determinations, imply guilt, or offer financial advice. Where allegations exist, they are explicitly labeled and sourced. Where information is incomplete or disputed, those gaps are clearly identified.

All material is compiled from publicly available sources and timestamped to reflect the state of information at the time of publication. This archive is intended for use by journalists, researchers, legal professionals, regulators, and members of the public seeking an accurate historical record.

If you believe information is inaccurate, incomplete, or missing primary sourcing, corrections and source submissions are welcomed.
